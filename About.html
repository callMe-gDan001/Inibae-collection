Our Story

Where tradition meets contemporary elegance

The Inibae Journey

Founded in 2023 by fashion enthusiast and cultural advocate Inioluwa Adesina, Inibae Collections was born from a deep desire to celebrate and preserve the rich textile heritage of West Africa. What started as a small workshop in Lagos has blossomed into a movement celebrating African craftsmanship on the global stage.

Every piece we create is a testament to the skill of our artisans, the vibrancy of our culture, and our commitment to sustainable, ethical fashion. We don't just sell clothing – we share stories, preserve traditions, and empower communities.

Our Milestones

2015

The Beginning

Inibae Collections was founded with just three artisans and a vision to celebrate African textile heritage. Our first collection featured 15 unique Ankara pieces.

2017

International Recognition

Our designs were featured at Lagos Fashion Week, gaining international attention. We expanded our artisan network to 25 skilled craftspeople.

2019

E-Commerce Launch

Launched our online platform, making authentic African fashion accessible worldwide. Shipped our first international orders to the UK and USA.

2021

Sustainability Initiative

Introduced our zero-waste production model and partnered with local communities to create sustainable dyeing practices using natural materials.

2024

Global Expansion

Opened showrooms in London and New York, bringing our heritage to the global fashion capitals while maintaining our roots in Lagos.

Our Core Values

Authenticity

Every piece is crafted using traditional techniques passed down through generations, ensuring genuine African heritage in every stitch.

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards in fabric selection, craftsmanship, and finishing to deliver pieces that last for generations.

Sustainability

Committed to eco-friendly practices, from natural dyes to zero-waste production, protecting our planet for future generations.

Community

We invest in artisan communities, providing fair wages, training, and opportunities that uplift entire families and regions.

A Note from Our Founder

"Growing up surrounded by the vibrant markets of Lagos, I was captivated by the stories woven into every fabric. Each pattern, each color held meaning – celebrations, heritage, identity.

Inibae Collections is my love letter to African craftsmanship. We're not just preserving tradition; we're reimagining it for the modern world. Every piece we create honors our past while embracing our future.

Thank you for being part of this journey. When you wear Inibae, you carry forward centuries of artistry and countless stories of resilience, creativity, and pride."

Folake Adeyemi

Founder & Creative Director
